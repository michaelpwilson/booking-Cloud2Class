computing cpd courses

Our in-school CPD courses are based on the popular Raspberry Pi computer. We will bring a training enviroment with us and set it up for you; All you have to do is provide the workstations*

For a little extra, you can even keep the Pi's to take home at the end.

For larger groups we have an online training environment providing virtual machines equivalent to a Raspberry Pi**, all available through any web-browser-capable device.

Our courses are for teachers or students. Just tell us which you want.

For people who want to learn a little more about technology and have some fun into the bargain.
This 2-hour workshop will introduce you to:

  • The Raspberry Pi computer
  • Ways to use the Pi desktop
  • Basic Python programming
  • Operating system and network concepts

During the two hours, we will show you how to set up and use a Raspberry Pi while putting together a small-scale social-media "Pi Project".

At the end of the workshop, you should have some ideas about your own projects, concepts for lesson-plans, and understand a little more about what this little machine can do.

We also provide Weekly Workshops over 5 or 12 sessions.

    5-session courses:
  • Operating System fundamentals with Raspbian
  • Project-coding: Python or Scratch basics
  • Fundamentals of networking
    12-session courses:
  • Networking, The Internet, and The Cloud
  • Project-coding: Python or Scratch
  • The Linux Operating System
